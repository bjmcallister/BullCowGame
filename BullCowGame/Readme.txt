Bulls and Cows

The Problem
-Mental Challenge
-Feel Smart
-Miss word puzzles
-Want a challenge

Concept and Rules
-Guess the isogram
-Isogram has no repeating guesses
-User has limited number of guesses
-After each guess we get Bull- right letter in right place or Cow - right letter in wrong place
-You win by guessing the word within max tries

Requirements
-Plain text intruction for all interactions
-Code to help make valid guesses
-Code to check number of bulls and cows
-code to keep track of valid guesses

Future Ideas
